About

Let me introduce myself.

I am a physiotherapy student at UBC graduating in September 2019 and looking for new opportunities. I have years of experience running my own personal training business and have a specialty certification in pre and post-natal fitness and coaching.
I am passionate about pelvic floor physio and women's health and aim to find employment in this field. I have completed my Pelvic Floor 1 and 2 courses to further my education and have pursued student placement opportunities where I can practice my skills in pelvic floor therapies.
I am constantly consuming new information and learning how to improve my skills and I am thrilled to be pursuing a career in the fast-paced and quickly growing field of physiotherapy.
